RockPaperScissors
Instructions
Rules:
1 v 1 fight between two players.
Each player is equipped at the start of the game with a weapon: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
This is displayed at the bottom of your screen in your respective corner.
For instance, if a player with Rock attacks the other player who also has Rock, then a small amount of damage is dealt.
If a player with Rock attacks the other player who has Scissors, then bonus damage is dealt.
If a player with Rock attacks the other player who has Paper, then no damage is dealt.
This applies to all other combinations -- standard Rock, Paper, Scissors rules apply (mostly lol).
There are pickups scattered around the map for different weapons.
If the enemy has a weapon that counters, run away and try to pick up a weapon that counters theirs.
When a players Health reaches 0, the game is over and will restart.
Player 1
  • Movement: W,A,S,D
  • Jump: H
  • Shoot: G
Player 2
  • Movement: Up, Down, Left, Right - Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Right Shift
  • Shoot: Right Control
